Your Sanctuary: Casa San Juan

Nestled in the historic village of San Juan del Obispo—home to the 17th-century palace of Francisco Marroquín, the first bishop of the Americas—Casa San Juan is your intimate retreat center just 15 minutes from Antigua, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Behind vine-covered walls, discover a lush garden, an architecturally enchanting main house, and six unique, modern rooms and lofts. The friendly staff ensures your comfort, safety, and serenity.

As a dedicated yoga and Ayurveda retreat center, Casa San Juan offers the perfect balance of tranquility and cultural immersion.

7-Night Itinerary: June 27 – July 5, 2026

Saturday, June 27 – Arrival & Opening Circle

Private transfer to Casa San Juan. Settle into your room. Enjoy a light vegetarian dinner featuring seasonal squash, black beans, and handmade tortillas. Gather in the garden for an opening celebration—sharing intentions under the stars.

Sunday, June 28 – Village Roots

7:00–8:30 AM: Gentle yoga & meditation. 9:00–10:00 AM: Full breakfast with local coffee, tropical fruits, and chia pudding. 10:00 AM: Walking tour of San Juan del Obispo—visit the historic palace and village plaza. 1:00 PM: Lunch at La Casa de Marc (5-star vegetarian-friendly). Afternoon free for journaling, painting, or rest. Light dinner at Casa San Juan.

Monday, June 29 – Antigua Soul

Morning yoga & breakfast. 10:00 AM: Historic walking tour of Antigua with renowned guide Elizabeth Bell. 1:00 PM: Lunch at Santo Domingo Hotel (vegetarian options). Free time to explore cobblestone streets, galleries, and courtyards. Light dinner at Casa San Juan. Evening free.

Tuesday, June 30 – Highland Villages

Morning practice and breakfast. Full-day excursion to local Mayan villages. Visit artisan homes, observe traditional weaving, and share lunch at a family-run comedor featuring organic vegetables and plantains. Return by evening. Light dinner.

Wednesday, July 1 – Artisan Market & Deep Rest

Morning yoga & breakfast. 10:00 AM: Antigua Artisan Market—meet textile weavers, ceramicists, and natural dye artists. 1:00 PM: Lunch at Café Condesa. Afternoon free. 6:00 PM: Light dinner. 8:00 PM: Yoga Nidra for deep restoration.

Thursday, July 2 – Cooking with the Land

Morning practice and breakfast. 10:00 AM: Visit a local produce market with Jean to select fresh ingredients. 2:00–5:00 PM: Hands-on vegetarian cooking class at Casa San Juan—prepare pepián (nutty vegetable stew), tamales colorados, and atole. Share your creations for dinner.

Friday, July 3 – Iximche: Sacred Grounds

7:00 AM: Coffee and departure. 8:30 AM: Breakfast at El Pedregal. 9:30 AM–1:30 PM: Guided tour of Iximche Mayan Ruins—capital of the Kaqchikel Maya. 2:00 PM: Lunch at Cabana Suiso with mountain views. Return to Casa San Juan by 7:00 PM.

Saturday, July 4 – Nourish & Celebrate

Morning yoga & breakfast. Optional spa treatments (massage, herbal steam). 5:00 PM: Farewell dinner at Mesón Panza Verde—multi-course vegetarian feast with wine pairing. Share stories, gratitude, and sisterhood.

Sunday, July 5 – Gentle Departures

Final sunrise yoga, meditation, and breakfast. Private airport transfers based on flight times. Carry home not just memories—but a renewed spirit.

Nourishment: Vegetarian Guatemalan Cuisine

All meals are 100% vegetarian, prepared with local, seasonal ingredients, and aligned with Ayurvedic principles—balancing taste, digestion, and energy.

Expect: pepián de calabaza, jocón de hongos, tamales colorados, atol de elote, fresh tropical fruits, and handmade tortillas.

Vegan, gluten-free, and high-protein options available upon request.
