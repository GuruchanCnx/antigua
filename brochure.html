Return to What Matters

This is more than a retreat—it’s a homecoming. For eight days in June, you’ll be held in a circle of just 11 women, guided by Jean Amabile and Ginger Hooven at Casa San Juan.

Casa San Juan is nestled in the historic village of San Juan del Obispo—home to the 17th-century palace of Francisco Marroquín, first bishop to the Americas—just 15 minutes from Antigua, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Meet Your Guides

Jean Amabile

73-year-old American wellness guide and Ayurvedic nutritionist. Certified Yin Yoga teacher and Tibetan sound bowls practitioner. Jean met Ginger in Antigua in 1999, and their shared vision has blossomed into this intimate retreat.

Ginger Hooven (Kamalesh)

Founder of Casa San Juan, seasoned yoga therapist and Ayurvedic practitioner. Her teaching weaves classical yoga with somatic movement and conscious breathwork.
